KOREE
PERRY WELCOME TO MY PORTFOLIO

Koree Perry is a student in the Philip Merrill Journalism School at the University of Maryland, College Park. Throughout her time at UMD, she has been engaged in numerous organizations on campus, such as the Black Student Union, the Maryland Promise Program, and Mind Matters. She also served as Co-Vice President and Journalism Lead for Cultoure Magazine during its first year of production. In her free time, Koree enjoys creative writing, reading, fashion and cooking. She hopes to make a career out of writing, as well as her wardrobe.
